MISSION AND VISION

Mission

OUR School provides an environment and highest academic standards in which every student discovers and realizes his full potential. Each student's self-esteem is fostered by positive relationships with students and staff. We strive to have our parents, teachers, and community members actively involved on our students' learning..

Vision

The MS GOBAL School aims to be an institution of excellence, dedicated to produce leaders of the future who care for the Humanity and Environment by accepting and loving varieties in our world.

Rules and Regulation

  •   who come to school by their own transport should arrive at the school before the school time. No student is allowed to come to school in self-driven scooter, motorcycle or car.
  •   All students are expected to arrive on time to the school. Students coming after 9.00am will not be allowed to attend the class. If late, he/she will be allowed only for the next session. The late comers have to sit in the library or in the lobby.
  •   Change of classrooms between periods should be done in silence and in an orderly manner.
  •   Students should be clean and neatly dressed. The school uniform is to be worn everyday and for all school functions. Students who are sloppily dressed or in un-uniform will be sent home.
  •   Students are advised to keep their classroom/school building/campus as neat possible and throw the leftovers only in the dustbins.
  •   No book (other than text books or library books), magazines or money should be brought to school without the Principal's permission.
  •   The parents are not supposed to send money through servants, drivers and attenders for any purpose.
  •   No gold ornaments and trinkets are allowed to be worn in school.
  •   The school will not be responsible for the loss of any goods. It is advisable not to bring valuable articles to schools.
  •   Students who come to school with the guardian should never leave before the guardian arrives. In case of delay, they should report to the school office.
  •   It is the sole responsibility of parents/guardian for the safety and security of the students who go home alone.
  •   First term fee should be cleared on or before the end of April and Second Term should be cleared on or before the end of September.
